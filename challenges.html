Data visualisation challenge: Native woodlands

This challenge will require the use of data manipulation, plotting and mapping skills, and is the culmination of the WIZ OF DATA VIS course stream. Scroll for more information on your tasks and how to complete the challenge

Challenge outline and objectives

While Scotland is best known for its heather hills, it used to be covered in forests, from which remain scattered patches of native woodland. (REWILDING AND CONTEXT......)

Data overview

You will use the following datasets:
- Native woodland survey data
- Native woodland survey (accompanying species structure data)
- National Nature Reserves outlines
